Frequently Asked Questions

1. What courses are offered in QIS College?

B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, MCA and Diploma courses are offered.

2. Is QIS College Autonomous?

Yes. QIS College of Engineering & Technology is an Autonomous Institution.

3. Is hostel facility available?

Yes. Separate hostel facilities are available for boys and girls.

4. What are the college working hours?

Monday to Saturday : 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM.

5. Does the college provide transportation?

Yes. Bus facilities are available from Ongole and nearby towns.

6. Is there a placement cell?

Yes. Every year leading companies visit the campus for placements.

7. Which companies recruit students?

TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Capgemini, Cognizant, HCL and many more.

8. Is there a digital library?

Yes. Students can access books, journals and digital resources.

9. Are scholarships available?

Yes. Government scholarships are available for eligible students.

11. Does the college have Wi-Fi?

Yes. The campus is fully Wi-Fi enabled.

Visit the admission office or contact the college through the Contact Us page.
